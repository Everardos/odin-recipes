Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 box chocolate cake mix
  • 1 package instant chocolate pudding
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 4 eggs, slightly beaten
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tsp. coffee mixed into 1/2 cup of water
  • 12 oz package of chocolate chips
  • Chocolate syrup to taste
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Use a stand mixer to mix all ingredients, except chocolate chips and chocolate syrup, for 5 minutes on low speed
  3. Add chocolate chips and stir them into the mixture with a spoon
  4. Spray bunt pan with no stick spray
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes
  6. Wait for cake to cool before removing from pan
  7. Drizzle with chocolate syrup
  8. Serve and enjoy
